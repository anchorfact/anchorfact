## TL;DR

Basketball is a 5v5 court sport invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith. Teams score by shooting ball through opponent's 10ft-high hoop. FIBA governs international play (Olympics, World Cup); NBA is the premier professional league. Key rules: 24-second shot clock (FIBA) / 14-second reset, 8-second backcourt rule, 5 personal fouls (FIBA) / 6 (NBA) per player.

## Core Explanation

Scoring: field goal = 2 points (inside arc), 3 points (outside arc), free throw = 1 point. Game duration: FIBA 4×10min quarters, NBA 4×12min. Positions: PG (point guard), SG (shooting guard), SF (small forward), PF (power forward), C (center). Violations: traveling, double dribble, carrying, backcourt, shot clock. Fouls: personal, technical, flagrant, unsportsmanlike. Substitutions: dead ball only.
