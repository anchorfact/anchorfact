## TL;DR
Basketball Rules and Fundamentals: Basketball rules define court play, scoring, timing, fouls, violations, possession, and player conduct.

## Core Explanation
Rule details vary by league, but core basketball rules cover teams trying to score by shooting into a basket, point values for shots, dribbling and traveling limits, personal fouls, and game timing.
