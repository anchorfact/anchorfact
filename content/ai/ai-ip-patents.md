## TL;DR
AI IP patents sit at the intersection of patent filings about AI technology and patent rules for inventions assisted by AI systems. The reliable public record comes from patent offices and WIPO, not from broad claims that AI automatically owns, replaces, or invalidates patent rights.

## Core Explanation
Patent analytics can track AI as a field of invention, while patent law must still decide who counts as an inventor and how AI-assisted work is disclosed or examined. WIPO reports on AI patenting trends across technologies and jurisdictions. USPTO materials track policy guidance and resources for AI-related intellectual property questions, including inventorship guidance for AI-assisted inventions.
