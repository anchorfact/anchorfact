## TL;DR
Stress Management Techniques: Stress management uses behavioral, cognitive, social, and physiological strategies to reduce harmful stress responses and improve coping.

## Core Explanation
Good stress guidance avoids miracle claims. Evidence-backed strategies include problem solving, social support, mindfulness-based practices, sleep, exercise, and seeking professional help when stress is severe or persistent.
