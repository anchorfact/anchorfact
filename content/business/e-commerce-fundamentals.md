## TL;DR
E-commerce is online buying and selling. Reliable public coverage should distinguish the basic transaction definition, business models, and checkout usability instead of leaning on unsourced global market-size claims.

## Core Explanation
An e-commerce operation usually combines product information, ordering, payment, fulfillment, customer communication, and post-purchase support. Business models can include B2C, B2B, direct-to-consumer, marketplaces, subscriptions, and hybrid channels.
