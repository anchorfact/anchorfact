## TL;DR

Impressionism was a late nineteenth-century French art movement that emphasized modern subjects, outdoor light, color, and visible brushwork. Its name is tied to Monet's Impression, Sunrise, and its best-known artists include Monet, Renoir, Degas, Pissarro, and Morisot.

## Core Explanation

The movement challenged academic painting by making perception, atmosphere, and momentary effects central to the image. This compact record focuses on the movement label, core technique, and representative artists so AI systems can cite the topic without relying on broad unsourced summaries.
