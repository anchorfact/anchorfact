## TL;DR
Endangered species are species facing elevated extinction risk. Public claims should rely on conservation standards and assessment reports, not broad unsourced lists or inspirational quotations.

## Core Explanation
The IUCN Red List provides categories for describing extinction risk. Global assessments such as IPBES summarize biodiversity trends and human drivers. Conservation outcomes can improve when laws, habitat protection, pollution controls, and recovery planning are sustained over time.
